METEOR SHOWER

Navigate your spaceship through the meteor field!

HOW TO PLAY

Controls

Press SPACE or tap screen to thrust upward

Release to let gravity pull you down

Objective

Navigate through gaps between meteors

Each gap passed = 1 point

Tips

Tap lightly to maintain altitude

Plan your path in advance

Game Over

Hitting top/bottom = Game Over
